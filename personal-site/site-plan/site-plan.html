Content

Home page

Our goal: As a self-taught hobby baker, I love to find and experiment with recipes. My family and I have enjoyed baking and cooking together since I was small, and I have learned so much from all of them. I will always remember those joy-filled afternoons sitting on the kitchen counter to peek into my mother’s stand mixer, or the happy evenings rolling cookie dough balls with my father. I was often asked how I made specific recipes or what was in the treats I made. This website is my attempt to effectively answer these questions, and have a little fun baking too. I hope you find as much joy and showstopper potential in these recipes as I have.

Recipes

Foolproof Chocolate Chip Cookies (Tags: Dessert, Cookies, Nostalgic)

  • Ingredients:
  • 2/3 cup shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 t vanilla
  • 1 t salt
  • 1 t baking soda
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 bag of chocolate chips

Instructions: Cream together your shortening, butter, eggs, brown sugar, white sugar, and vanilla until fully combined. In a separate bowl, combine baking soda, salt, and flour. Add this mixture a little at a time and start mixing! Add the full bag of chocolate chips, and mix until evenly distributed. Roll or scoop your cookie dough balls and then bake in a preheated oven for 8 - 10 minutes @375*
