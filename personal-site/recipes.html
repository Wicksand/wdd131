Foolproof Chocolate Chip Cookies

Tag

Tag

Tag

Title

  • 2/3 cup shortening
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 t vanilla
  • 1 t salt
  • 1 t baking soda
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 bag of chocolate chips
Instructions: Cream together your shortening, butter, eggs, brown sugar, white sugar, and vanilla until fully combined. In a separate bowl, combine baking soda, salt, and flour. Add this mixture a little at a time and start mixing! Add the full bag of chocolate chips, and mix until evenly distributed. Roll or scoop your cookie dough balls and then bake in a preheated oven for 8 - 10 minutes @375*
